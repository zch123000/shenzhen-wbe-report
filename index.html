7Key Findings & Scientific Insights
1
Clear Inflection Point in Epidemic Decline (Oct 2022 → Apr 2023)
Dual-target positive rate declined continuously from the Oct 2022 peak of 38.7% to 0% in May–Aug 2023, perfectly tracing the Omicron epidemic recession curve. April 2023 had the largest sample volume (552), yet maintained a 15.9% dual-target rate.
2
Stronger Early Epidemic Signal in Luohu District
Luohu District showed higher dual-target positive rates in October (42.9%) and December (39.3%) 2022 compared to Futian (35.3% / 11.1%), indicating Luohu as an early-warning sentinel has priority monitoring value. Futian spiked to 37% in January 2023, slightly later than Luohu.
3
N Gene Is More Sensitive Than ORF1ab
N gene detection rate (54.0%) significantly exceeds ORF1ab (31.7%). Among positive samples, mean N Ct = 35.18, ORF1ab = 34.87, with high correlation (r ≈ 0.92). N gene is optimal as the primary screening target.
4
Median Ct Values Reveal Low Viral Load
N gene Ct median = 36.3 (range 6.9–44.7); 75th percentile at 38.7, indicating generally low virus concentration near the detection limit — consistent with high dilution in wastewater matrices.
5
High Positive Rate at Water Treatment Plant
Binhe Water Treatment Plant (21.8%), Futian Bonded Area Pump Station (20.8%), and Huanggang Street (20.0%) show the highest dual-target positive rates, consistent with the principle that wastewater treatment plants aggregate upstream population and amplify viral signals.
6
IC Failure Rate Requires Attention (26.4%)
IC pass rate is only 73.6%, dropping to 33-39% during May-July 2023, suggesting high-concentration PCR inhibitors or reduced extraction efficiency. Magnetic bead enrichment preprocessing has key value in addressing this issue.
8Detection Methods & Quality Control
样本采集
Wastewater samples were collected from pumping stations and water treatment plants in Pingshan, Futian, and Luohu districts of Shenzhen, with a volume of 1000 µL. Key Operational Points

  • Wastewater sample stored at 4°C; centrifuged (5000×g, 30 min) to remove large particles
  • Proteinase K pre-treatment degrades proteins and reduces PCR inhibitors
  • After magnetic beads bind to nucleic acids, magnetic rack achieves solid-liquid separation
  • 4-tube merge wash: concentrate 4×4 mL samples to 50–80 µL
  • Final concentration factor approx. 200–320×, significantly improving detection sensitivity
  • Total process: 2 h (automated) / 4 h (manual)

Ultrafiltration Tube Enrichment (Comparison Method)

  • Meizheng Enrichment Instrument with Ultrafiltration Concentration Tube
  • Soak in 500 mL wash solution; repeatedly aspirate with syringe to enrich virus
  • Elution: immerse bottom in elution buffer, aspirate 20 times
  • Proteinase treatment optimized incubation time: 10/20/30 min
  • PBS buffer assists in improving proteinase activity

Compared to magnetic bead method: ultrafiltration suitable for large volume (500 mL), magnetic bead method for smaller volume (4–16 mL). Both require Proteinase K pre-treatment.< mL), magnetic bead method for smaller volume积（16 mL），两种方法互补覆盖不同场景

Yixin 4in1 Four-in-One Method (Innovation)

In Step 4 (five-well) of the standard extraction procedure, pause the program, merge the four wells containing magnetic beads into the fourth well, then resume after sufficient enrichment.

  • Magnetic bead amount: 20 µL; Proteinase K: 20 µL
  • Lysis buffer: 3.6 mL; sample: 4 mL / well
  • Four-well merge: equivalent to processing 16 mL sample
  • Compared to general reagent, detection limit improved approx. 4×

Method Limitations

  • Kit limited to specified sample types (wastewater matrix)
  • Improper sample collection/transport/storage may cause false negatives
  • Cross-contamination during sample processing may cause false positives
  • Samples below LoD (< 10 copies/mL) may not be detected
  • Wastewater matrix contains PCR inhibitors, which may cause IC failure (IC Ct > 40)

IV. Limit of Detection (LoD) Validation Study

Validation Experiment Design

  • Test Object: SARS-CoV-2 Pseudovirus Nucleic Acid Standard Sample
  • Spiked Concentration: 100 copies/mL (Reference)
  • Dilution Gradient: 1 : 0.1 : 0.05 : 0.025 : 0.0125
  • Corresponding Concentration: 100, 10, 5, 2.5, 1.25 copies/mL
  • 50 mL per sample, total 6 validation samples
  • Technical Replicates: n = 2 per RNA sample
  • Background Control: Negative Wastewater Sample

Comparative Validation Design

  • 方案 A：通用磁珠核酸提取试剂盒（行业参考）
  • 方案 B：项目组研制磁珠直接提取法
  • After both extraction methods, universal qPCR detection was applied
  • Detection Volume: 16 mL / sample (4×4 mL)
  • Commissioned by: Shenzhen Second People's Hospital
  • Validation Date: Dec 8–11, 2023
